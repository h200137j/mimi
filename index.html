My Lunavere

Maybe I'm being petty. I kinda feel I am. Maybe I'm over reacting. But I'm angry with myself, I'm angry at the world. I hate how I have fallen for you, yet I have loved every moment of it. Euphoria. A dream I do not want to end.

In my head we grow old together, have 2 kids, they are all grown now, so it's just you and I in a big house. It's funny but there are some rooms we haven't been in for a while now.

I don't blame you for not wanting me the way I want you. I tend to go a bit extra when I have fallen in love. Go over board. Make grand gestures etcetera etcetera. But I'm all grown now. I recognize the difference in our emotions, lest I fall upon a sword and do something I will regret, I need to step back.

Perhaps you are right, maybe there is a future where I say hie one day and we go on like nothing ever happened. That would be nice.

I love you, I want to spend my every moment with you. Close. To have the honor of being loved by you. To stand before all in heaven and in earth as they stand witnesses to the love we blossom. Maybe it's a fantasy. I think I moved too fast. That's on me. Something I need to work on. But I love you now and always. There is an immutable space in my heart for you, for as long as the earth remains.

I will miss you, I think of you everyday. Replaying memories, holding your hand while I drive. Your hugs, your fragrance. The way my tummy did things at the sight of you. My lunavere. I will miss you more than you can fathom. And I will love you always.

Know you are loved.
